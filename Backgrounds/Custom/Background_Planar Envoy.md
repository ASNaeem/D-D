# Planar Envoy

Source: Custom Background

You serve as a diplomat, messenger, or emissary between the Material Plane and the outer planes of existence. Perhaps you were chosen by a celestial archon to carry messages of light to mortal kingdoms, or you bargained with a fiendish power to serve as their advocate in mortal affairs. You have walked the shifting streets of Sigil, negotiated with genie lords in the Elemental Planes, or navigated the labyrinthine courts of the Abyss and Nine Hells. This experience has given you a deep understanding of planar politics, cosmic lore, and the unique languages and customs of the multiverse's inhabitants.

**Ability Scores:** Charisma, Strength, Wisdom

**Feat:** [Alert](../../Feats/Feat_Alert.md) or Musician (Chosen: [Alert](../../Feats/Feat_Alert.md))

**Skill Proficiencies:** Insight and Religion

**Tool Proficiency:** Choose one [Musical Instrument or Gaming Set](../../Equipment/Equipment_Tool.md)

**Languages:** Infernal or Abyssal

**Equipment:** Choose A or B: (A) One musical instrument or gaming set (same as above), Traveler’s Clothes, Scroll Case (with letters of introduction), a small cosmic token (e.g., a shard of Celestian crystal or piece of Obsidian), 15 GP; or (B) 50 GP
