# Javelin

Source: Player's Handbook (2024)

*Simple Ranged Weapon*

- **Cost:** 5 sp
- **Damage:** 1d6 Piercing
- **Properties:** Thrown (Range 30/120)
- **Weight:** 2 lb.
- **Mastery:** Slow (If you hit a creature, its Speed is reduced by 10 feet until the start of your next turn).

A light spear designed primarily to be thrown.
