# Longsword

Source: Player's Handbook (2024)

*Martial Melee Weapon*

- **Cost:** 15 gp
- **Damage:** 1d8 Slashing (One-Handed) / 1d10 Slashing (Versatile)
- **Properties:** Versatile
- **Weight:** 3 lb.
- **Mastery:** Sap (If you hit a creature, it has Disadvantage on its next attack roll before the start of your next turn).

A classic two-handed or one-handed sword with a crossguard, favored by knights and paladins.
