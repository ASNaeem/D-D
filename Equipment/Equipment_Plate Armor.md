# Plate Armor

Source: Player's Handbook (2024)

*Heavy Armor*

- **Cost:** 1,500 gp
- **Armor Class (AC):** 18
- **Strength Requirement:** Strength 15
- **Stealth:** Disadvantage
- **Weight:** 65 lb.

Plate armor consists of shaped, interlocking metal plates to cover the entire body. A suit of plate includes gauntlets, heavy leather boots, a visored helmet, and thick layers of padding underneath. Buckles and straps distribute the weight over the body.
