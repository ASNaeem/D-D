# Chain Mail

Source: Player's Handbook (2024)

*Heavy Armor*

- **Cost:** 75 gp
- **Armor Class (AC):** 16
- **Strength Requirement:** Strength 13
- **Stealth:** Disadvantage
- **Weight:** 55 lb.

Made of interlocking metal rings, Chain Mail includes a flexible undercoat of quilted fabric and leather to prevent chafing and cushion the impact of blows.
