# Holy Symbol

Source: Player's Handbook (2024)

*Adventuring Gear (Spellcasting Focus)*

- **Cost:** 5 gp
- **Weight:** 1 lb.

A Holy Symbol is a representation of a god or pantheon. It can be an amulet depicting a symbol of a deity, a badge engraved or characteristically shaped as a holy symbol, or a symbol carefully painted on a shield. A Paladin can use a Holy Symbol as a spellcasting focus for Paladin spells.
